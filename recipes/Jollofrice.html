Jollof Rice

Description

This is a Dish that is very popular in the West African Region. It has a rich and a mix of flavour that makes you longing for more!!!

Ingredients

  • Rice: 3 cups of long-grain parboiled or Basmati rice.
  • The Base: Red bell peppers, Scotch bonnet peppers (ata rodo), tomatoes, and onions.
  • Oil: Vegetable or canola oil for frying the sauce.
  • Tomato Paste
  • Seasoning: Thyme, curry powder, bay leaves, and bouillon cubes (Maggi/Knorr).
  • Chicken or beef stock.

Steps

  1. Blend your tomatoes, red bell peppers, Scotch bonnets, and half an onion until smooth.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot. Saut√© sliced onions, then add tomato paste and fry for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the blended pepper mix. Cook on medium heat until the water reduces and the oil starts to rise to the top.
  4. Stir in your thyme, curry powder, bouillon cubes, and bay leaves.
  5. Wash your rice thoroughly to remove excess starch. Add it to the pot along with the chicken stock.
  6. Cover the pot tightly with foil and a lid. Cook on low heat so the rice steams in the sauce rather than boiling in water.
  7. Once the rice is soft and the liquid is gone, stir in some fresh tomato slices or butter for extra shine.
